ABOUT SUNDOWN TUBING TESTING
Sundown Tubing Testing was founded in 1998 which later incorporated in 2006 to Wally's Equipment Inc., dba Sundown Tubing Testing. Our company is located in Sundown, Texas, where we provide quality service to our customers. We also operate from Coahoma, Texas with all services available.

Hydrostatic Testing and Scanalog- Our Hydrostatic Testing is relied upon to find leaks and weak joints on 1 1/2" to 3 1/2" tubing. Our testing is completed on douline, plastic, saltaline, and regular pipe coatings. The testing department consist of 7 experienced testers. Our Scanalog department consist of five scanners experienced in searching for rod ware and pitting with the most up to date scanning system.

Roustabout- We repair flow leaks, high pressure injection leaks, infect pumps, change belts, speed up and slow down wells, lower & raise rods on wells, work on test satellites all around.


Sundown Tubing Testing is contracted with several surrounding companies including Apache, Occidental Permian, Tema, Exxon Mobil Corporation, Texland Petroleum, Hess Corporation, Chevron, Juno Energy, H.L. Brown, Nordstrom Engineering, Endeavor, XTO and many more.
